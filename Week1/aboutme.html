Ultimate Frisbee

I can and will talk your ear off about Ultimate Frisbee. You all should definitely join the team at UCLA -- it is a great community and an absolute blast. A lot of folks haven't heard of or played frisbee before, so here's a rundown of the 2 main positions in the game and what they entail:

  1. Cutter
    • Cutters do a lot of the running. They'll often generate a lot of distance, get thrown to, and then throw the disc to a handler.
    • Probably more tiring, since you're running longer distances.
    • Pro: You get the glory of scoring.
    • Con: You have to run fast in order to score it and running is tough sometimes.
  2. Handler
    • Handlers do a lot of the throwing, of moving the disc.
    • Pro: You get to throw it really far which is fun.
    • Con: If you throw it poorly, or if the defender blocks your throw, it's a big bummer.
